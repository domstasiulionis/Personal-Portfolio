About Me

Hey, I'm Dominykas Stasiulionis (Dominic), I am a front-end developer and software engineering graduate from the University of Greenwich. I started off primarily working with Java and Python to develop desktop apps of various complexity. Over time, I noticed that I would always enjoy developing the front-end of an application a lot. I often volunteered myself for the role in any programming-related group work at university. After graduating, I decided to pursue front-end development as a serious full-time career.


I have experience using various modern-day front-end tools and technologies such as: JavaScript, React and SASS/SCSS to name a few. Paired with my disciplined work ethic, I utilise these tools and technologies to meet requirements efficiently and correctly. Outside of the realm of front-end, I am also very familiar with agile/scrum methodologies. I have experience participating in and leading various types of meetings and I am not afraid to take on new responsibilities outside of my day-to-day ones, if required.


Overall, I am a driven team-player who is dedicated to progress and success. I always strive to meet set requirements and go above and beyond to ensure that the most optimal results are achieved.
